My Secondary School



This is my Secondary School which is Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Selinsing. This is an unforgettable memories that still alive in my mind. In this school there are many shappy and sad moments but always the happy moments come first into my mind. What makes this school happy for me is because of my friends. They are a good friends for me. Of course, school is boring but thanks to my friends who make it a place that full of happiness and laughs. I really miss my secondary school friends because they are the best friends that I ever had. Eventhough, sometimes I faced problems but my friends always give me support and tell me not to give up.

The best moments that I still remember is when we go to the Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM) together with a bus to attend the University Carnival. At the USM we learned the correct ways in order to apply the best University to ourself after the important exam which is Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia or also known as SPM. I am a Science Stream student but to be honest our batch is not so good in our exam results. However, we always support each other in order to improve our exam results. For an example, we study together, we make some simple notes and distribute it to all of our classmates. Normally, the one who do that things are female students but we also do not forget to our male classmates. We also shared what we got to each other and when we have leisure time in class, all of us will study together. To be honest, they are simple memories but they are unforgettable for me. I really hope that we can see each other again one day.
