Sometimes, I do realize that our parents miss the old times when all of us are together. I am also sad because, i rarely spent time with my parents because of my thick schedule. I realize that my father and mother are becoming old and old now and sometimes I am sad if I imagine they are not by my side anymore. However, I always remember to pray good things to my family because I really love them. We have gone through many obstacles and challenges in our life but we never fail to support each other. One of my precious memory about my family is when they celebrate my birthday on previous July this year. They sang a song and pray for me. I love my family so much...
